A B2B SaaS startup needed a complete visual overhaul to support their transition from beta to public launch. The project included a redesigned marketing site, sales deck, case study templates, and a library of product illustrations that simplified complex enterprise workflows.

**Deliverables:** Marketing site design, sales deck, 30+ product illustrations, case study templates, social assets.

**Impact:** Post-launch, the redesign contributed to a 40% increase in free trial signups.
