A set of custom botanical illustrations for AUREA BATH's product storytelling. Each illustration was inspired by the natural ingredients in their products — lavender, eucalyptus, rose, and chamomile — rendered in a style that balances scientific precision with artistic warmth.

**Deliverables:** 12 botanical illustrations, ingredient story artwork, website hero illustration, print pattern designs.

**Impact:** The illustrations became a core part of the brand identity, featured across packaging, web, and in-store displays.
