A complete brand identity for a growing e-commerce platform specializing in curated home goods. The brief called for a visual system that felt premium yet approachable — trustworthy enough for high-value purchases, warm enough to feel personal.

**Deliverables:** Logo system, color palette, typography, packaging design, social media templates, brand guidelines.

**Impact:** The rebrand contributed to a 25% increase in brand recall during their subsequent campaign.
