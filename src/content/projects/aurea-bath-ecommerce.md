A complete visual overhaul of AUREA BATH's e-commerce presence — from product page layouts to checkout flow illustrations. The goal was to create a shopping experience that felt as premium as the products themselves, reducing friction while elevating the brand.

**Deliverables:** Product page UI kit, category page layouts, checkout illustrations, promotional banners, email template system.

**Impact:** Post-launch, the site saw a 22% reduction in bounce rate and a 15% increase in average order value.
